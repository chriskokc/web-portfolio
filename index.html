ABOUT.

My name is Chris and I am a Junior Software Developer. I used to study Economics in Hong Kong. After spending two years of working in the financial industry, I decided to pursue something I will enjoy working for the rest of my life, and by chance I found out that I am passionate about coding! I really enjoy it as it requires both creactivity and technical knowledge. I aspire to create something useful for other people and make an impact to our society.


Therefore, I flew to the United Kingdom as there are many different opportunities in the tech industry. I joined the Nology Tech Consultancy Programme, and it has been one of my best decisions. It is my first time to meet different people who share the same passion as mine, and those real-life projects has sharpened my knowledge and skills in programming. More importantly, I learnt to contribute my code to the entire team. With passion, I am unstoppable!
